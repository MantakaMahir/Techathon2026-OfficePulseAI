# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Office managers, team leads, and the boss who already uses Discord as the daily command center. They need a quick way to see which office lights and fans are still running, especially before leaving for the day or when checking remotely.

## Product Purpose

OfficePulse AI monitors 3 office rooms with 15 corrected v1.2 devices: each room has 2 fans and 3 lights. It keeps one backend source of truth for simulated device state, live power usage, alerts, dashboard visuals, and Discord bot answers. Success means judges can see the same changing state on the web dashboard and in Discord without hardcoded answers.

## Brand Personality

Technical, alert, trustworthy. The product should feel like a compact control room: clear enough for a non-technical boss, credible enough for judges evaluating AI and IoT engineering.

## Anti-references

Do not look like a generic smart-home clone, a static spreadsheet, a fake hardcoded bot demo, or an overdecorated SaaS landing page. Avoid login screens, admin panels, and features that do not help prove the rubric.

## Design Principles

Show one source of truth everywhere.
Make waste visible immediately.
Prefer demo reliability over speculative complexity.
Use AI to humanize facts, never to invent readings.
Treat Wokwi as IoT proof, not as a fragile dependency for the live demo.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, reduced-motion support for rotating fans and pulses, and text labels alongside color-coded states so alerts and device status do not depend on color alone.
